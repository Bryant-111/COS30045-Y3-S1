Televisions

This page focuses on television energy consumption and the key factors that affect it.

Screen Size and Energy Consumption

One of the most important factors affecting energy consumption is screen size. Larger TVs use more electricity because they require more power to display images.

This means that choosing a larger TV will generally result in higher energy usage.

Screen Technology

Different screen technologies such as LCD, LED, and OLED affect performance and energy use. LCD and LED TVs are more common because they are cheaper and widely available.

OLED TVs are less common because they are more expensive, even though they provide better image quality.

Buying Considerations

  • Choose the right screen size for your needs
  • Check energy labels before buying
  • Consider long-term electricity costs
