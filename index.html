Why energy consumption matters

Household appliances contribute significantly to electricity use. Understanding how products differ in energy consumption helps consumers make better buying decisions and can reduce long-term energy costs.

Televisions are a good example because their energy use is influenced by factors such as screen technology, screen size, and usage patterns.
